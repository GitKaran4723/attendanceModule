Our Journey

Department History

A young department with a vision for the future.

2024

Department Established

The BCA Department was established at Bangalore University with a vision to provide world-class education in computer applications. Started with 60 students and 10 faculty members.

2024

Modern Lab Setup

State-of-the-art computer labs were established with the latest hardware, high-speed internet, and licensed software for practical learning.

2024

Industry Partnerships

Signed MoUs with leading IT companies for internships, guest lectures, and placement opportunities for students.

2025

Growing Strong

Expanded to 200+ students with new batches, enhanced curriculum covering AI/ML, and launched student-led tech projects like this Attendance Management System.
