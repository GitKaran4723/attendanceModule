How to Apply

Simple 4-Step Admission Process

Follow these easy steps to secure your seat in our BCA program.

Online Application

Fill out the online application form with your personal and academic details. Upload required documents.

Document Verification

Submit your original documents for verification. Our team will review and validate your application.

Merit List & Selection

Selection is based on 12th grade marks. Merit list will be published on the official website.

Fee Payment & Enrollment

Pay the admission fee online or at the college. Collect your ID card and start your journey!

Requirements

Eligibility Criteria

Make sure you meet the following requirements before applying.

school Educational Qualification

Must have passed 10+2 or equivalent examination from a recognized board with minimum 45% marks (40% for reserved categories).

menu_book Subjects Required

Mathematics/Business Mathematics/Statistics as one of the subjects in 10+2. Computer Science as a subject is an advantage.

calendar_today Age Limit

No specific age limit. However, candidates should have completed their 10+2 in the current or previous academic year.

public Nationality

Indian nationals and NRI candidates are eligible. Foreign nationals may apply under the NRI quota with valid documents.
